Final Semester at SDSU

Reflection of my time at University, Oct 2023

Entering my last semester at San Diego State University, I'm brimming with gratitude and anticipation. These years have been a whirlwind of growth and learning. With a focus on Finance & Information Systems, I'm excited to apply my skills to real-world projects.

Beyond academics, leadership roles in organizations like Alpha Kappa Psi have enriched my education. As I approach this milestone, I'm eager to absorb every last bit of knowledge and make the most of my time on this vibrant campus. Here's to a memorable final semester and the exciting chapters ahead!
